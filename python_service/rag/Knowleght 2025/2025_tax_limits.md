## Child Tax Credit - 2025

| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|

| Refundable portion (ACTC) | Up to $1,700 |
| Income phase-out begins (Single) | $200,000 |
| Income phase-out begins (MFJ) | $400,000 |

### Requirements for Child Tax Credit:
- Child must be under 17 at end of tax year
- Must be your dependent
- Must have valid SSN
- Must have lived with you 6+ months
