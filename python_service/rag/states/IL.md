# ILLINOIS TAX RULES 2025
# Illinois Department of Revenue

## OVERVIEW
- Tax Type: FLAT TAX
- Rate: 4.95%
- Social Security: NOT TAXED

## FLAT TAX RATE
**4.95% on all taxable income**

Illinois Constitution requires flat tax.

## PERSONAL EXEMPTION
- $2,775 per person
- $2,775 per dependent

## RETIREMENT EXEMPTION
- Social Security: NOT TAXED
- Government pensions: NOT TAXED
- Private retirement: NOT TAXED

## ILLINOIS EIC
- 20% of federal EITC
- Refundable credit

## COMMON QUESTIONS

Q: What is Illinois tax rate?
A: 4.95% flat rate on all income.

Q: Does Illinois tax Social Security?
A: No, Social Security is exempt.

Q: Does Illinois tax retirement?
A: No, most retirement income is exempt including pensions, 401k, IRA.
