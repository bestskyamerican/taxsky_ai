# NEW YORK TAX RULES 2025
# NY Department of Taxation and Finance

## OVERVIEW
- Tax Type: Progressive (9 brackets)
- Top Rate: 10.9% state + 3.876% NYC = 14.776% max
- Social Security: NOT TAXED

## YONKERS TAX
- Residents: 16.75% surcharge on NY state tax
- Non-residents working in Yonkers: 0.5% on wages

## PENSION EXCLUSION
- Government pensions: Up to $20,000 excluded
- Private pensions: Fully taxable
- Social Security: 100% excluded

## COMMON QUESTIONS

Q: Does NY tax Social Security?
A: No, New York does NOT tax Social Security.

Q: Do I pay NYC tax if I work in NYC but live in NJ?
A: No, NYC tax only applies to NYC residents.

Q: What's the top NY tax rate?
A: 10.9% state (over $25M) + 3.876% NYC = 14.776% combined max.
